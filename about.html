About PYCH

Empowering Papua's youth through creativity, technology, and innovation since 2019

Our Story Watch Video

Our Journey

Papua Youth Creative Hub (PYCH) is a collaborative movement founded in 2019 by 21 indigenous Papuan youth with the blessing of President Joko Widodo. PYCH serves as a platform for developing creativity and innovation among Papua's youth, preparing for Indonesia's Golden Generation.

We focus on youth entrepreneurship (UMKM), creative industries, millennial farmers, socio-cultural activities, education, and health. Through PYCH TOP.ID, we specialize in technology assembly and repair services for laptops and smartphones by Papuan hands.
